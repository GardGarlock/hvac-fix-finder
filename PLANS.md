> **Purpose (for AI agents):** This document describes a B2B service offering for electrical contractors: positioning, problems solved, product pillars, ICP, integrations, pricing, differentiation, and business goals. Use it as the source of truth for copy, features, and scope boundaries—not as an implementation spec unless a task explicitly references building this product.

## Overview

We help electrical contractors recover lost revenue through automation systems that improve lead response, follow-up, and customer reputation management.

## The Problem

Most electrical companies lose jobs because:

- Calls go unanswered during peak hours.
- Estimates are never followed up on, leaving money on the table.
- Review requests are inconsistent, hurting local SEO.
- Old leads are forgotten, wasting previous marketing spend.

## The Solution

We solve these problems with a simple, repeatable, and automated system.

---

## The Core Offer

### 1. Missed Call Recovery

When a customer calls and no one answers, the system instantly triggers:

- **Automated text responses:** Immediate engagement to stop them from calling a competitor.
- **Follow-up messages:** Keeping the conversation warm.
- **Estimate booking prompts:** Moving the lead directly into the calendar.

**Goal:** Recover missed opportunities before the customer calls another electrician.

### 2. Review Automation

Automatically requests Google reviews from completed jobs via:

- SMS and email reminders.
- Automated multi-step follow-ups.

**Goal:** Increase online reputation, improve Google rankings, and generate more inbound calls.

### 3. Lead Follow-Up & Reactivation

Automated workflows designed for:

- **Pending estimates:** Nudging customers to sign.
- **Inactive leads:** Re-engaging old inquiries.
- **Maintenance reminders:** Creating recurring service opportunities.

**Goal:** Generate additional revenue from leads the company has already paid for.
