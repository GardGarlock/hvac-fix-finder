The Revenue Leak

Most electrical companies lose jobs after the lead already arrived.

The phone rings, the estimate is sent, the job is completed, or the old inquiry goes quiet. Without a system, those moments depend on whoever remembers to follow up.

01

Calls go unanswered

Peak hours, after-hours demand, and busy office staff create missed opportunities that often call the next electrician.

02

Estimates stall

Open quotes sit in the CRM without a clear follow-up cadence, leaving approved work on the table.

03

Reviews are inconsistent

Happy customers are not asked at the right time, which weakens local search visibility and trust.

04

Old leads are forgotten

Past inquiries and maintenance opportunities go untouched even though the business already paid to generate them.
